Chicken Stir-Fry

Quick and healthy meal full of flavor

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast (sliced thin)
  • 1 red bell pepper (sliced)
  • 1 carrot (sliced)
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan.
  2. Add chicken and cook until golden.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 3â€“4 minutes.
  4. Pour in soy sauce, honey, and garlic.
  5. Stir well and cook for 2 more minutes. Serve hot with rice.
