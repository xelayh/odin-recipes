Chocolate Chip Cookies

Crispy on the outside, soft inside

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup flour.
  • ½ cup butter (softened)
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ tsp baking soda
  • ½ cup chocolate chips
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Mix butter and sugar until creamy.
  2. Add egg and whisk well.
  3. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt.
  4. Fold in chocolate chips.
  5. Scoop onto a baking tray and bake at 180°C for 10–12 minutes.
