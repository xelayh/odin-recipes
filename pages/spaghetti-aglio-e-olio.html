Spaghetti Aglio e Olio

Simple Italian pasta with garlic and olive oil

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic (thinly sliced)
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • Salt
  • Fresh parsley (chopped)
  • Grated parmesan (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and fry garlic until golden.
  3. Add chili flakes and a bit of pasta water.
  4. Mix in the drained spaghetti and toss well.
  5. Add parsley and parmesan before serving.
